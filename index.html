About IYS Boisar

ISKCON Youth Services (IYS) Boisar is the blazing torch of ISKCON, dedicated to forging unbreakable spirits through Lord Krishna’s teachings. Led by Shri Parthasakha Das Prabhuji, we infuse Hindutva’s sacred fire into youth, cultivating fearless character, spiritual strength, and unshakable devotion to Bharat’s eternal culture.

History: The Eternal Flame

A Legacy of Revival

Bharat’s youth today echo the rebellion of 1960s America, lost in a haze of materialism and despair. Like Arjuna on Kurukshetra, they seek truth. His Divine Grace A.C. Bhaktivedanta Swami Prabhupada, a lion of Vedic wisdom, lit the path for lost souls, reviving the eternal dharma of Krishna Consciousness. His legacy burns bright in IYS Boisar, shattering myths that the Vedas are bound by time or place.
